Hand in Hand

Natural Disaster Donation Registries

Hand in Hand is a platform that connects individuals directly to relief organizations with maximum transparency


Problem

With the decrease in government aid, more and more people rely on donations after a natural disaster strikes. However, people often donate items that are not needed, which creates more logisitcal challenges[1] and delay in aid[2]. Our platform solves this problem with transparency by combining registries with relief organizations. Anyone who visits the site can select a natural disaster site and see which items are most needed by the victims. They can choose to contribute online through Amazon Smiles or give a monetary donation. With each donation, contributers can see first-hand as the disaster site grows closer to their goals.
